# Georgia DPH District 6 Health Indicators Dashboard

A public health analytics project completed during my Master of Public Health (Health Informatics) internship with the **Georgia Department of Public Health – East Central Health District (District 6).**

## Overview

This project involved developing a county-level health indicator database, creating geographic visualizations, and producing comprehensive health profiles for all 13 counties within Georgia Department of Public Health District 6.

The goal was to transform publicly available health data into actionable information that could support public health planning, community health assessments, and evidence-based decision-making.

---

## Objectives

- Develop a centralized county health indicator database
- Analyze demographic and health outcome trends
- Create GIS maps to visualize geographic disparities
- Produce county health narratives
- Support public health planning with data-driven insights

---

## Selected Findings

Examples of notable findings include:

- Geographic variation in chronic disease burden across District 6
- Higher rates of cardiovascular disease in several rural counties
- Elevated diabetes mortality in Jenkins County
- Significant differences in cancer mortality between counties
- Lower infant mortality rates in Columbia County compared to surrounding counties

These analyses highlighted important health disparities that can inform future public health initiatives and resource allocation.
